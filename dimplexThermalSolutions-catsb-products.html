DIMPLEX THERMAL SOLUTIONS

Koolant Koolers offers a variety of sizes ranging from fractional ton to 1000′s of tons of cooling, with standard or custom designs for virtually every cooling application. Koolant Koolers can build a system to cool any type of liquid, in any equipment, to any specification. The design, reliability and service of the Koolant Kooler chiller are what makes it the choice for cooling applications.
Koolant Koolers can be found in many markets, including many industrial applications such as machine tool, plating, die casting, laser, plastics and any other where a liquid needs to be cooled. The industrial market is especially hard on equipment with its the variety of contaminants such as oil mist, metal particles, dust and severe heat, but the Koolant Koolers chillers are built to withstand the conditions with optimal performance. Koolant Koolers chillers are engineered and are built with reliable components and quality workmanship.
Our medical market chiller is well known for its dependability, which is essential for cooling critical hospital equipment. With a consistent 99% uptime, our medical chillers are the ideal choice when reliability is the critical.
