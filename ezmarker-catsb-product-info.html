Pulsed series is equipped with the most advanced fiber laser technology laser for marking on IC.

The air-cooled Pulsed series system has a compact dimension so that it can be integrated into any existing handling system or in-line production. Together with its long lifetime and maintenance-free operation , the Pulsed series will deliver the best quality marking.

Features:

  • Fiber delivery
  • Air-cooled
  • Small-spot, single mode marking beam
  • Environmentally sealed laser
  • Small footprint
  • No consumables
  • Maintenance free
  • High reliability
  • Guide laser (red)

Ezmarker CW series is the laser marker for the electronics industry. This continuous wave fiber laser marker has been primarily designed to mark molded housings of discrete and integrated components with high quality and in the shortest of time. Thus, the system codes on smallest parts with highest resolution and accuracy.

In addition to the single-scan-head marking system, Exceltech offers a dual-scan-head system with almost twice the marking field of a single-head system.

Features:

  • High resolution
  • Reliable
  • Integrates easily, flexibly and quickly into any production environment
  • Compact design (small footprint)
  • Mount in any orientation
  • Air cooled
  • Zero maintenance
  • Low cost of ownership
  • Flexible and scalable OEM system for high volume marking

The EZ Marker Fiber Laser implemented on the TM-8002 System is specifically engineered for marking semi-conductor components in JEDEC trays. Single and dual head versions are available.

The solid state laser source and fiber beam delivery system provide the advantages of low energy consumption and extremely long operating life.

Features:

  • Completely self-contained air cooling system reduces overall size and complexity
  • Mark definition and contrast quality are excellent on most semiconductor package materials.
  • Tray “banking” feature on the handler assures precise, consistent mark alignment with the component edges.
  • Windows compatible EZMARK ll software is extremely user friendly, making set-up of marking parameters fast and easy.
